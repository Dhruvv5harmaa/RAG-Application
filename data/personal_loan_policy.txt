Personal Loan Policy

Section 1: Loan Amount
The minimum personal loan amount is INR 50,000.
The maximum personal loan amount is INR 25,00,000.

Section 2: Loan Tenure
The minimum tenure for a personal loan is 12 months.
The maximum tenure for a personal loan is 60 months.

Section 3: Interest Rate
Personal loan interest rates range from 10.5% to 18% per annum.

Section 4: Prepayment
Prepayment is allowed after 6 months from the date of disbursement.
A prepayment charge of 2% applies on the outstanding principal.
